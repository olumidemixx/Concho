About CONCHO

Sap Concho is inspired by the endangered Puerto Rican crested toad, a species native to Puerto Rico and an integral part of its natural heritage. As part of our mission to give back to the community and protect our planet, we’re teaming up with the talented artist behind the Sapo Concho character to make a real-world impact. We’re currently working to identify a local conservation effort dedicated to preserving this remarkable species and its habitat. Stay tuned for updates on our partnership and how you can join us in supporting this meaningful cause. Together, we can ensure the future of Sapo Concho—both in the crypto world and in the wild.
